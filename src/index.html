»WOMB« is lucid underwater music composed by Kajsa Lindgren and was released 15/06/18 on Hyperdelia. This is a virtual experience of »WOMB«, turn your speakers on and take a dip.

»WOMB« is a musical narration for abstracted ears and bodies – engulfing a listener simultaneously in subaquatic sonic environments, distant dreams of childhoods, memories and voices from the unknown: where time and space fold into each other. Read more about »WOMB«.

Please note: This web installation is best experienced on modern desktop or mobile devices and headphones.
